SUMMARY

In the near future, cities are expected to become increasingly walkable as individuals and governments grow more conscious of the need to address pollution from transportation. The necessity for adaptable and durable backpacks is thus increasing rapidly.

NEOKånken uses a lightweight exoskeleton frame to keep its shape and distribute weight comfortably over long wear. A snap-on system adds or swaps straps, padding, pockets, and accessories, while durable, weather-resistant materials keep weight down—creating one customizable, organized carry for any day.
