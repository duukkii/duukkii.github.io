Pixel Paths

Accessible career exploration for children

SUMMARY

Pixel Paths is a gamified digital platform designed to close the opportunity gap in career exploration for children aged 7–11. Inspired by the rising demand for equitable education technology, it provides interactive career discovery through quizzes, learning quests, reflective journaling, and an AI companion (“Pixel Bot”). The system encourages children to design their own futures by visualizing diverse career paths, building confidence, and breaking stereotypes. With scalable features like vision boards, milestones, and inclusive modes for PWDs and ADHD learners, Pixel Paths aims to foster early aspirations while ensuring accessibility and long-term impact.
