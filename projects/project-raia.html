PROJECT RAIA

Rapid Adaptive Intelligence Agent

60.003 Product Design Studio, SUTD EV Club

Category

  • Product (Automobile) Design
  • 3D Modelling & Rendering
  • Graphic Design
  • Video Editing

Summary

RAIA is an autonomous EV-charging vehicle for Singapore’s EV-majority future. Deployed 24/7 in every carpark, it autonomously navigates to parked EVs and delivers rapid energy via modular battery “PIPs.” This turns any lot into an EV bay without new infrastructure, cutting land use and queues while boosting accessibility and scalability. The result is fast, seamless, hands-off charging within minutes.
