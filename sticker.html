SUMMARY

RAIA is an autonomous EV-charging vehicle for Singapore’s EV-majority future. It solves the demand-supply gap by bringing charging to the car. Deployed 24/7 in every carpark, RAIA autonomously navigates to parked EVs and delivers rapid energy via modular battery “PIPs.” This turns any lot into an EV bay without new infrastructure, reducing land use and queues while boosting accessibility and scalability. The result is fast, seamless, hands-off charging within minutes.
