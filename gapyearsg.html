SUMMARY

@gapyearsg is a Singapore-based Instagram platform dedicated to guiding and supporting students through their gap year journeys. The account shares curated resources, personal stories, tips, and structured guides to help “gappers” make meaningful, intentional use of their year off. Featured topics include planning, portfolio-building, mental wellness, mentorship, and real-world experiences. The initiative, started by a medical student who experienced the challenges of finding credible gap year guidance, aims to dispel misconceptions and foster a supportive, informed community.
