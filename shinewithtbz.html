CATEGORY

SUMMARY

#5hinewithtbz was a community fan-event to celebrate k-pop group, THE BOYZ's 5th anniversary, which was hosted at K Land Cafe (GR.iD, Singapore) in 2022. My role as lead designer meant that I created the full event visual identity and all attendee-facing collateral: two cupsleeve designs, photocard set, event gifts redemption poster, price list, and social updates.
