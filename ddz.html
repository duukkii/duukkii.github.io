SUMMARY

I served on the 24/25 EXCO of SUTD’s Hip Hop club Dance DerivativeZ, leading external publicity and marketing. I managed Instagram & TikTok (@sutd_ddz), ran day-to-day Stories, designed campaign assets, and produced merchandise and show collateral for Ignition (2024) and Byte 4.0 (2025)—expanding my skills in brand design, social strategy, and end-to-end production.

SUMMARY

Ignition is an annual showcase competition hosted by DDZ to give our dancers, especially the freshies, an opportunity to perform on stage. I contributed in terms of ideating shirt designs, posters, as well as producing the programme booklet for the production.
