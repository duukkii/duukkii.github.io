Secretary of SUTD ROOT's Media & Marketing Department (EXCO)

SUTD ROOT (Student Government)

CATEGORY

  • Graphic Design
  • Marketing
  • Social Media Management
  • Illustration
  • Video Editing

SUMMARY

I served on the 25/26 EXCO as the Secretary of Media & Marketing in SUTD's student government, ROOT. As the head of the marketing team, my role was to oversee and guide my directors to design good marketing collateral for SUTD's events and socials (@sutdlife on ig and tiktok). However, before I took on the role of the Secretary, I was first a Director myself, designing collaterals for school events and running the social media accounts.
