SUMMARY

As Publicity Head, I led the end-to-end visual system for Camp Maranatha 2023: from the event identity to web, social, on-site collateral, and merch. I built and maintained the Wix site for sign-ups/updates, produced the camp booklet and wayfinding, and created cohesive keepsakes for participants. Additional deliverables included sticker sheets, Crocs jibbitz, lanyard pattern/cards, postcard set, camp shirt artwork, and a multi-page programme booklet prepared for print..
